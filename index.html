📖 Buffer Setup Guide

        

          
1. Go to buffer.com and create a free account.

          
2. Connect your social accounts (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Facebook, etc.).

          
3. Go to buffer.com/developers → create a new app.

          
4. Copy your Access Token and paste it in the Buffer setup above.

          
Note: Buffer's free plan supports up to 3 channels. Upgrade for all 6 brands.
